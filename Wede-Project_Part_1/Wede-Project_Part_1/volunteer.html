Volunteer With Us

Join our team of amazing volunteers and make a difference in the lives of animals.

Volunteers help with walking dogs, feeding, cleaning kennels, and events.

Why Volunteer?

  • Make a real difference in animals' lives
  • Gain valuable experience in animal care
  • Meet like-minded animal lovers
  • Flexible scheduling to fit your lifestyle
  • Community service hours for students

Volunteer Opportunities

Dog Walking & Exercise

Help our dogs stay happy and healthy by taking them for walks and playtime in our exercise areas.

Animal Care & Feeding

Assist with feeding animals, cleaning kennels, and providing basic care and comfort to our rescued pets.

Event Support

Help at adoption events, fundraisers, and community outreach programs to spread awareness about animal welfare.

Administrative Support

Assist with paperwork, answering phones, social media updates, and other office tasks.
