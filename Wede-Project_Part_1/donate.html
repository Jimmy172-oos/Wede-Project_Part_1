Support Our Work

Your donations help us provide shelter, food, and medical care to animals in need.

Every contribution, no matter how small, makes a real difference in the lives of rescued animals.

Why Your Donation Matters

R100 Can Provide

  • Food for 5 dogs for one day
  • Basic medical supplies
  • Bedding for kennels

R500 Can Provide

  • Vaccinations for multiple animals
  • Emergency veterinary care
  • Spay/neuter procedures

R1000+ Can Provide

  • Major surgery for injured animals
  • Long-term care for special needs pets
  • Rescue operations and transportation
