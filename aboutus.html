About Us

The Bloemfontein SPCA is dedicated to preventing animal cruelty and helping animals find safe homes. Our mission is to provide shelter, rescue, and advocacy for animals in need.

Animal Welfare Services

  • Animal Rescue: We respond to reports of animal cruelty and neglect
  • Medical Care: Full veterinary services including emergency care
  • Spay/Neuter Programs: Helping control pet overpopulation
  • Adoption Services: Matching pets with loving families
  • Lost and Found: Helping reunite lost pets with their families

Community Education

  • School vistis to tech children about animal care
  • Pet care workshops for new pet owners
  • Community outreach programs
  • Responible pet ownership education
Emergency Services
  • 24/7 animal emergency hotline
  • Wildlife rescue and rehabilitation
  • Animal cruelty investigation
  • Disaster response for animals
